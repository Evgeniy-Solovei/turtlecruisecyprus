Founded in 2015 by a team of passionate divers and nature enthusiasts, we've grown from a small boat operation into Ayia Napa's premier turtle-watching experience.

Our mission is simple: to connect people with the wonders of the Mediterranean Sea while promoting sustainable tourism. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, a family on vacation, or a couple seeking romance, our cruises offer something magical for everyone.

We don't just talk about protecting the environment; we live it. Our catamaran runs on low-emission engines, and we use biodegradable products onboard. We partner with organizations like the Cyprus Marine Environment Protection Association to monitor turtle populations and clean up beaches. Guests are encouraged to participate in "leave no trace" practices, like using reef-safe sunscreen we provide. By choosing SCUBACAT, you're supporting eco-tourism that benefits both wildlife and the community.

Our Story: From Passion to Premier Cruises

It all started with a love for the sea. Our founders, avid scuba divers, noticed the abundance of sea turtles around Cape Greco and wanted to share this hidden gem responsibly.

What began as informal outings evolved into structured cruises on our custom catamaran, designed for comfort and eco-friendliness. Today, we operate daily morning and sunset trips, boasting a 90%+ turtle sighting rate. We're proud to have hosted over 10,000 guests, creating lifelong memories while supporting local marine conservation efforts.
