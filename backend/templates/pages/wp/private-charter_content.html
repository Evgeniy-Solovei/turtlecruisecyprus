Private Charter

Private charters out of Ayia Napa Harbour.
No fixed schedule, no strangers, no compromises.

We run daily cruises for the public. But sometimes a group deserves something different — a full catamaran or other yacht to themselves, a crew that answers only to them, and a day that goes at their pace.

That's what a private charter with us is.

You get the same 60ft catamaran, the same Captain Jimmy who's spent 35 years on these waters, and the same route through Cape Greco Marine Reserve — Blue Lagoon, Sea Caves, Love Bridge, Konnos Bay. What you don't get: anyone else on board. No shared deck. No strangers at lunch. No itinerary you didn't agree to.

We adapt the route, the timing, and the BBQ to your group. Proposing? We know the spot. Big family reunion? We've done it. A birthday for someone who'd never book this for themselves? Perfect reason.

The boat fits groups of up to 30. It's stable on open water, has two decks, and gets close enough to the sea caves that your guests will be telling the story for years. The BBQ is cooked fresh before we leave harbour — chicken souvlaki, grilled halloumi, tzatziki — and adjusted for dietary requirements without a second thought.

Pricing depends on group size, timing, and what you want the day to look like. There's no online booking form for this one. Just send us a message on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out properly.
