Beyond Turtles: The SCUBACAT Experience

SCUBACAT TURTLE CRUISE isn't just about turtles; it's about the entire experience. We've crafted a journey that caters to a younger, vibrant audience (20–45 years old) looking for more than just a boat trip. Imagine gliding across the shimmering Mediterranean Sea on a sleek catamaran, the sun warming your skin, and the salty breeze carrying whispers of adventure.

This isn't your average sightseeing tour—it's a curated escape where wildlife encounters blend seamlessly with relaxation, social vibes, and a touch of luxury. At SCUBACAT, we believe that true magic happens when you connect with nature while forging unforgettable memories with like-minded explorers.

The Heart of the Adventure: Our Unique Catamaran Design

What sets SCUBACAT apart starts with our vessel itself. Our modern catamaran is engineered for both comfort and agility, allowing us to venture into hidden coves and shallow bays that larger boats simply can't access. Picture this: spacious decks with plush loungers, shaded areas for those midday siestas, and an onboard BBQ setup that turns every morning cruise into a floating feast. The design prioritizes stability, so even if you're prone to seasickness, you'll feel at ease as we navigate the crystal-clear waters around Cape Greco.

But it's not just about the hardware—it's the thoughtful details that elevate the experience. We provide high-quality snorkeling gear free of charge, ensuring everyone can dive in without hassle. Our crew, a mix of seasoned sailors and marine enthusiasts, are always on hand to share insights about the local ecosystem. They're not just guides; they're storytellers who bring the sea to life, pointing out schools of colorful fish, hidden underwater caves, and, of course, the star attractions: the ancient sea turtles that call these waters home.

Tailored for the Young and Adventurous

Our cruises are designed with a youthful spirit in mind. If you're between 20 and 45, you'll find the vibe perfectly attuned to your energy—think upbeat playlists drifting from the speakers, opportunities for spontaneous group games, and plenty of space to mingle with fellow travelers.

We attract a crowd of wanderlust-driven individuals: solo adventurers seeking serenity, couples on romantic getaways, and groups of friends chasing Instagram-worthy moments.

Take our Morning Cruise, departing at 9:00 AM and wrapping up by 1:30 PM. For just 40€ (including BBQ), you get a full itinerary packed with action. We set sail from Ayia Napa Harbour, heading straight to Turtle Bay, where sightings are guaranteed on over 90% of trips. Here, you'll have ample time to snorkel alongside loggerhead and green turtles, marveling at their graceful movements. Then, it's off to Blue Lagoon for a refreshing swim in turquoise waters that look like they've been photoshopped. The BBQ lunch—featuring fresh grilled meats, salads, and local Cypriot specialties—serves as the perfect interlude, fostering conversations and laughter among passengers.

For those who prefer golden-hour magic, our Sunset Cruise (16:00–20:00, 35€) offers a more laid-back affair. We cruise to Konnos Bay, a protected haven teeming with marine life. As the sun sets, painting the sky in hues of orange and pink, you'll sip on complimentary drinks while watching turtles surface for air. It's romantic, reflective, and utterly photogenic—ideal for capturing that viral reel.

We attract a crowd of wanderlust-driven individuals: solo adventurers seeking serenity, couples on romantic getaways, and groups of friends chasing Instagram-worthy moments.

More Than Wildlife: Building Connections and Sustainability

Beyond the turtles, SCUBACAT emphasizes holistic wellness and environmental stewardship.

We partner with local conservation groups to promote sustainable tourism, educating guests on how to minimize their impact. Simple actions like using reef-safe sunscreen (which we provide samples of) make a big difference in preserving these fragile habitats.

The social aspect is equally compelling. Many guests leave with new friendships, bonded over shared thrills like spotting a turtle pod or jumping off the catamaran into the sea. We've heard stories of proposals at sunset, birthday celebrations turned epic, and even impromptu dance parties on deck. It's this community feel that keeps people coming back—our repeat rate is over 70%, a testament to the addictive blend of adventure and chill.
