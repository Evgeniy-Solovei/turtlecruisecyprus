We know where they are.

Turtle cruises out of Ayia Napa. Morning and sunset, every day of the season.

We run two daily eco-tours aboard our 60ft yacht-catamaran — purpose-built for the waters around Cape Greco Marine Reserve. Small groups, experienced crew, snorkelling gear on board. The route takes you through some of the most iconic spots on the Cyprus coastline: Love Bridge, Blue Lagoon, Sea Caves, Konnos Bay. And yes — 90% of our guests spot turtles.

We're fully licensed, eco-certified, and serious about leaving zero impact on the marine environment we work in. Our guides know Cape Greco the way most people know their own street — every current, every feeding ground, every place a loggerhead likes to rest. That's not a marketing line. It's just what happens when you spend years on the same water.

BBQ included. Free cancellation. Book online, show up, let the sea do the rest.
